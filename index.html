Welcome to the Crystal Collector Game!

Try to guess the Magic Number!

There are four crystals below, each with a different hidden value.

Click on one to add its value to your score! You win if you can get your score to match the Magic Number; you lose if your score goes above the Magic Number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

For an added challenge, try to win with as little clicks possible!

There's a new Magic Number and crystal values with each new game. Have fun!
